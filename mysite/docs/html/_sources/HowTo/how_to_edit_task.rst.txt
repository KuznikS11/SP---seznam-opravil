Howto edit a task
=====================


#) login; if you don't have an account yet, first register

#) click All tasks on your left side or click See all tasks on your right side

#) Depending on whether the task you want to edit is finished or unfinished, choose finished or unifinished tasks

#) Change informations about task in a form you see

.. image:: edit_task.png

#) Click Edittask to edit you task

#) Click Cancel if you changed your mind and you don't want to edit this task
